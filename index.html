Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

The kindergarten years (3-6) focus on early learning, social skills, and emotional development through play, exploration, and basic education.

Elementary School

Elementary school provides foundational education in subjects like math, reading, and science, fostering social, emotional, and cognitive development.

Middle (10-16) Years

The middle years (10-16) are a time of physical, emotional, and cognitive growth, marked by increased independence and identity exploration.
